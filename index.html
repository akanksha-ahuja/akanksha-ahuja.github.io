Hi there, I am Akanksha

I’m a Computer Scientist at CMS Offline Computing, at CERN, Switzerland. I manage, analyse and support the European grid sites of the WorldWide Computing Grid for production and analysis of the detector data.

Previously, I was a Technical Student at AWAKE, and had an immersive experience in the world of High Energy Physics. I helped the physicists in my team and developed a remote control magnet software application for the electron beam line for long term and consistent monitoring.

My experiences and efforts in my past and current research projects have stemmed out from uniting multiple disciplines, and an internal desire to build things that make this world a better place for everyone.
